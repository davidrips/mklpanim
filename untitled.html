Our mission is to empower students, parents, and educators.

Getting through school and into college is needlessly complicated and mysterious, and valuable information and educational resources are hard to find and filter through.

myKlovr uses machine learning and our specialized educational insight to provide students with personalized recommendations to make being a student easier.

We create a tailored, step-by-step plan to help students move towards their goals...

...whether that means getting better grades, getting a part time job, or getting into their dream school.

Advanced analytics and modeling helps students see their improvements in real time and make decisions about their next move.

Parents and educators can track their progress and collaborate to make sure students are getting the support they need.

We connect students and parents directly to pertinent resources, content, and services, college admissions departments, and expert insight.

We want to give students all the support they deserve, and give parents all the help they need.
